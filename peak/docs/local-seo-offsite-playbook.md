# Local SEO Off-Site Playbook (Suceava)

## 1) Google Business Profile

- Primary category: Internet cafe.
- Secondary categories: Video game rental service, Cafe (only if accurate).
- Service area: Suceava.
- Add booking URL: `https://peak-gaming.site/rezervare`.
- Add website URL: `https://peak-gaming.site`.
- Add contact URL: `https://peak-gaming.site/contact`.
- Add services/products:
  - PlayStation 5 stations
  - Gaming PC stations
  - Online reservation
  - Hourly pricing
- Post one update weekly with new photos and short offer text.

## 2) Review Acquisition

- Ask every satisfied player for a review after session checkout.
- Use a short QR code handout to the GBP review form.
- Ask for natural wording mentioning city and service (example: "PS5 in Suceava").
- Reply to all reviews in 24-72h with local/service phrasing.
- Target cadence: 4-8 new reviews/month.

## 3) Citation Consistency (NAP)

Use the exact same business details everywhere:

- Name: Peak Gaming
- Address: update to exact street and number used on-site
- Phone: update to public customer phone used on-site
- URL: `https://peak-gaming.site`

Priority citation targets:

- Google Business Profile
- Apple Maps
- Bing Places
- Local Romanian directories and city portals (Suceava)
- Social profiles (Instagram bio and website field)

## 4) Local Backlinks

- Partner with local schools/universities/esports groups.
- Publish event pages and ask partners to link to booking pages.
- Sponsor local gaming competitions and request homepage links.
- Pitch local media/community posts about tournaments and promos.

## 5) Monthly Checklist

- Update GBP photos and posts.
- Review NAP consistency across all profiles.
- Track reviews gained and average rating.
- Track backlinks gained (quality over quantity).
